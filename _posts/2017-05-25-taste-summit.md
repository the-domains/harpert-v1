# Charles van Goch

Charles van Goch will also be joining us at the TASTE Summit -- Executive Day! Charles van Goch is a Hotel Management School Maastricht graduate and the founder of Mise en Place. Mise en Place is an international leading employment agency specialized in the hospitality trade since 1994 and to be found in 7 countries with 55 offices. Van Goch is one large chunk of energy and he loves to share that energy with people. He gets his energy from talking to people, motivating and inspiring them. We can't wait to be inspired by him on Wednesday the 12th of July!
